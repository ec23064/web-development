My Top 5 Tourist Attractions

Niagra Falls

The attraction is a group of three warterfalls with the highest flow rate of any waterfall above 50m in North Mmerica, which is located on the border of New York, USA and Ontaria, Canada

The Eiffel Tower

The attraction is a 330m tower buily in 1887-1889, which is located in the center of Paris

Magic Kingdom

The attraction is the largest amusement park in the entire world, which is in Orlando, Florida and is owned by Disney

Machu Picchu

The attraction is an Incan citadel that rises to 2,430m above sea level and built in the 15th centure. It is located in the Andes Mountaines in Peru

The London Eye

The attraction is the tallest ferris wheel in Europe, which is on the South Bank of the River Thames in London
